Welcome to WGA Smash, a community for competitive Super Smash Bros. For Wii U!

We host weekly meetings in Maple Hall Room 217 every Thursday while school is in session from 5-10 PM. It is recommended to bring your own controller, but feel free to talk to an officer if you need to borrow one for the meeting.

Connect with us on Facebook, or watch our streams on Twitch!



New member? Or just want to scope out the competition? Check out the club member database and add in your own information!

Club Activities

We run a double-elimination bracket every week that's open to everyone (including non club members), usually starting at 7 PM.

It is 100% free to enter, with the exception of the $2 Power Ranking ("PR") brackets which are used to determine our quarterly player rankings. Enter these to prove your skill, and to contribute to the club so we can buy new equipment!

These are some of the brackets that we have run in the past:

  • Singles
  • Doubles
  • Crew Battles
  • Reverse Mains
  • CPU Doubles
  • Random Bracket

Additionally, setups are always available for friendly play during the whole club session!

Beyond our Thursday club meetings, you can find many additional smash tournaments on the UW campus and beyond. This calendar shows all the events that take place in Western Washington, courtesy of the WWA Facebook page.
